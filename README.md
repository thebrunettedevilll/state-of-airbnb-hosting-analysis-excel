# Survey Data Analysis
## Project Overview
This project presents an exploratory analysis of survey data, focusing on the demographic characteristics of respondents. The goal is to clearly communicate key patterns through visualizations and concise analytical insights.

## Data
- Source: Survey responses
- Format: Excel (.xlsx)
- Method: Data aggregation using pivot tables

## Visual Analysis

### Gender Distribution
![Gender distribution](visuals/gender_distribution.png)
**Insight:**  
The sample is male-dominated, with female respondents representing a substantial minority.

### Age Distribution
![Age distribution](visuals/age_distribution.png)

### Ethnicity Distribution
![Ethnicity distribution](visuals/ethnicity_distribution.png)

### Highest Level of Education
![Education distribution](visuals/highest_level_of_education.png)

**Insight:**

---

### Current Employment Status
![Employment status](visuals/current_employment_status.png)

---

### Household Income Distribution
![Income distribution](visuals/my_entire_household_income_before_taxes_in_2015.png)

**Insight:**  
Household income levels are distributed across multiple ranges, with a higher concentration in the lower to middle income brackets.

## Key Takeaways
- The survey sample is predominantly young and male.
- Respondents generally show moderate to high levels of education.
- Employment and income patterns suggest economically active participants.
